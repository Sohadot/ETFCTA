Signal horizon.

The disclosed lookback or time horizon governing a signal—not its evaluation or rebalance frequency.

Critical distinction

Daily evaluation does not establish a one-day signal horizon. Monthly rebalancing does not establish a one-month signal horizon.

Governed example

KMLM decision DEC-KMLM-SH-001 confirms that its signal horizon is not determinable from the reviewed sources.

Unknown is not empty

The finding records reviewed scope and prevents unsupported completion.
