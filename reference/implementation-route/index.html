Implementation route.

The disclosed path by which a fund obtains its strategy exposure—not a guess from a single holding or instrument.

Evidence requirements

The route may distinguish direct futures, futures and forwards, other derivatives, and controlled commodity subsidiaries. Permitted instruments are not automatically the governing route.

Governed examples

KMLM: direct futures with commodity subsidiary.

DBMF: mixed derivatives with commodity subsidiary. The record does not reduce this to swaps-only.
