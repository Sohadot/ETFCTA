Managed-futures replication.

A methodology seeking to reproduce material exposures or pre-fee behavior of a representative managed-futures set without implying index tracking.

ECS boundary

The result requires an explicit primary-source replication description. It must not be converted into “tracks a named index” unless that relationship is separately disclosed and admitted.

Governed example

DBMF decision DEC-DBMF-RM-001 is managed-futures replication. Its benchmark relationship remains not determinable as index tracking.

Not a quality label

Replication describes strategy construction, not expected effectiveness.

ETFCTA reference language

Benchmark relationship
