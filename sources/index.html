What a source proves.

Source authority is specific. No document type proves every aspect of a fund.

Prospectus

Can support: principal strategy, instruments, subsidiary use, objectives, and stated risks. Does not prove: undisclosed signal parameters or current positioning.

Statement of Additional Information

Can support: legal structure, governance, additional investment permissions, and subsidiary detail. Does not prove: that every permitted instrument is the governing implementation route.

Official holdings

Can support: a dated portfolio state and instrument presence. Does not prove: the full strategy universe, persistent implementation route, or signal horizon.

SEC filing metadata

Can support: filing identity, accession, dates, series, class, and legal record linkage. Does not prove: strategy claims absent from the filed document.

Issuer fund page

Can support: current issuer identity, public description, identifiers, and document discovery. Does not override: governing legal documents or resolve conflicts silently.

Admission rule

Each evidence record must name its source, bounded locator, supported ECS rule, admission state, and limitations.
