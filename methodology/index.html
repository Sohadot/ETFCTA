From source to publication.

ETFCTA separates documents, bounded observations, rules, decisions, review, and public output.

The governing chain

Source → Evidence → Rule → Decision → Review → Publication. Discovery alone never establishes a classification.

Classification boundary

ECS describes strategy structure and knowledge state. It does not rank funds, predict returns, or establish suitability.

Unknowns are results

When reviewed evidence cannot determine a value, ETFCTA publishes the bounded unknown rather than completing the record by inference.

Examples in release 0.4.0

KMLM leaves signal horizon undetermined. DBMF leaves index-tracking status undetermined.

Review

Every decision identifies its evidence, rule version, limitations, actors, and next review date.
