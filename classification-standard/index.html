ECS-001.

A versioned vocabulary for describing managed-futures ETF structure without turning structure into a score.

What ECS classifies

Active dimensions include strategy or replication method, management mode, market breadth, implementation route, directional flexibility, benchmark relationship, and signal horizon where evidence permits.

What ECS does not classify

Quality, expected return, risk-adjusted superiority, portfolio suitability, or a “best fund” result.

Decision semantics

A confirmed decision means the evidence conclusion passed the rule. It does not mean an unknown underlying value became known.

Version

ECS-001 v0.1.0 governs registry release 0.4.0.
