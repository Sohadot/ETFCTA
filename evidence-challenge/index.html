Challenge the evidence.

A public route for missing sources, newer documents, better locators, rule objections, and disclosure conflicts.

What to submit

  • Fund or decision ID.
  • Primary-source URL and document date.
  • Exact page, section, paragraph, or machine locator.
  • The claim supported, contradicted, or left unresolved.
  • The requested action: admit, supersede, correct, or review.

What happens next

A submission remains a candidate. ETFCTA verifies identity, preserves the source where possible, extracts bounded evidence, applies the governing rule, and records the outcome publicly.

No paid override

Issuer identity, professional status, or commercial relationship does not bypass evidence admission or correction governance.
