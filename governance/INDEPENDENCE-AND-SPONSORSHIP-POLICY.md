# Independence and Sponsorship Policy

Issuers, sponsors, advertisers, clients, and commercial licensees receive no control over rule design, evidence admission, decision status, review timing, corrections, comparisons, or result ordering.

Commercial relationships MUST be disclosed near the relevant commercial surface. Sponsorship MUST NOT create an approval badge, accelerate evidence admission, suppress limitations, or change classification. Classification staff and processes MUST be able to publish adverse, unknown, conflicting, corrected, or withdrawn states without commercial approval.
