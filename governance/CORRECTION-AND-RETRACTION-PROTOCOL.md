# Correction and Retraction Protocol

Errors are corrected through append-only events. The registry MUST preserve the incorrect published state, record when the correction was made, explain whether the cause was an ETFCTA error, new evidence, changed disclosure, changed fund, or changed rule, and point to the current decision.

A decision is withdrawn when its evidence is withdrawn, its governing rule is retired without a valid migration, a material conflict makes the result unsafe to retain, or the record fails a release gate. Withdrawal is not deletion.
