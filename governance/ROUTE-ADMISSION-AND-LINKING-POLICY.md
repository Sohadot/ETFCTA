# Route Admission and Internal Linking Policy

A public route is admitted only when it represents a complete reference object, an evidence-governed analysis, or a real professional workflow. Empty tickers, keyword variants, auto-generated performance lists, insufficient comparisons, and reordered duplicates are prohibited.

Admitted fund routes MUST link to their decisions, evidence, sources, changes, applicable ECS dimensions, glossary terms, strategy family, and implementation route. Reference routes MUST link back to admitted real examples when examples exist; until then they MUST state that the registry is in its founding state.
