# Known Limitations

Last reviewed: 18 July 2026

- No real fund has been admitted; category coverage is zero.
- The five active ECS rules cover only narrow founding cases, not the full ontology.
- The synthetic fixture proves mechanics only and is not evidence of production research quality.
- ETFCTA provides no investment recommendation, suitability judgment, performance forecast, or real-time data.
- Classifications will depend on public disclosures and may not reveal proprietary implementation details.
- Absence of disclosure does not establish absence of a feature.
- Signal horizon, counterparties, collateral, and other dimensions may remain unknown.
- Preservation backup and restore procedures are specified but not yet production-proven.
- A named operator identity, challenge intake channel, and independent second-review capacity are not yet public.
- Human/JSON parity and canonical-route checks are not yet automated because no fund dossier is published.
- No API, accounts, alerts, telemetry, paid product, or sponsorship surface exists.
