RegistryMethodology

Evidence must earn admission.

A discovered source is a candidate—not a published fact.

Admission states

candidate → identity_verified → preserved → extracted → admitted.

Minimum controls

  • Verify document authority and date.
  • Preserve a retrievable version and cryptographic hash where available.
  • Extract one bounded observation with an exact locator.
  • Apply a named active rule and derive sufficiency.
  • Record limitations, review date, roles, and publication state.

Rejection boundary

Marketing language, discovery snippets, and unsupported inference cannot silently become admitted evidence.
